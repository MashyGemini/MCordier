About Me

• Who I am and what I can do •

  • Who I am

    Hi, I'm Maché Cordier, I am 22 years old and currently still studying. I studied for graphic and print design as well as web and user interface design. I strive to work hard and develop myself more in everything I learn, create and accomplish.

  • Graphic Design

    The software and tools I developed for graphic design is Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, Dreamweaver, Flash and Adobe Acrobat. Handrendering and visual communication is a big part of me.

  • User Interface Design

    Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop are tools I mainly use as a base with next phase on to code, designing dashboards and user interface kits for a creative user experience.

  • Web Developent

    My knowledge in different languages would be HTML5, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery. I know different frameworks such as Bootstrap and can work with e-commerce sites and wordpress.

  • Multimedia

    I enjoy Multimedia and Motion Graphics and have fair amount of knowledge and skill in Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro. Same for sound editing in Adobe Audition and Reaper.

• Portfolio Website •

First portfolio ui design and web development

I wanted to stay with my portfolio design concept last year, where I did drawings and created a “story book like” feel and style.

My general idea of this design was to keep good open space and very clean and neat, nothing is too busy or even “boring” in this website. The UI-designs are different from the actual website because some of the elements on the designs itself did not bring justice to my website like I thought it would. I did not change my UI-designs, to show what I actually wanted before the changes.

• Ragnarök •

MTV top three identity project

The project started for us to pick a unique theme to showcase 3 music videos on a short string on MTV. We created a unique graphic identity for MTV, a more focussed identity for the countdown, and packaged it all together in Adobe After Effects. My concept was Ragnarök. The idea consists of many things such as, series of future events, including a great battle foretold to ultimately result in the death of a number of major figures and even the end of times or apocalypse. My message I wanted to bring over was of that but more that the end result will always look brighter and good. We as humans would have to watch over our planet better.
